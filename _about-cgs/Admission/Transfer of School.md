### **Transfer of School** 

Students who wish to transfer to the school at the beginning of the academic year for Secondary 2 or Secondary 3 may submit their interest via the Waiting List Form, which will be available from 1 to 30 October each year.

If you wish to submit an application, please return to this page on 1 October. All applications must be submitted by 30 October.

Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted by the school. Shortlisted candidates may be required to sit for placement tests and attend an interview.

For enquiries, please contact the school at **crescentgirl@moe.edu.sg**.
