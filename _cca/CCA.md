<h3><strong>CO-CURRICULAR (CCA) DEPARTMENT</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>VISION</strong></h4>
<p>Holistically developed students with good character and strong core values.</p>
<h4><strong>MISSION</strong></h4>
<p>To provide a broad-based co-curricular programme and fuel interest in
life-long recreational pursuits.</p>
<h4><strong>CURRICULUM</strong></h4>
<p>By the end of the 4 years of CCA, a Crescentian should be able to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Learn the value of responsibility, teamwork, discipline, and commitment;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Acquire social-emotional learning competencies and leadership skills;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop interest and talent in specific areas of CCA;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in inter-school competitions/external events.</p>
</li>
</ul>
